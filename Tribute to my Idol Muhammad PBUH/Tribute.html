The Prophet's life The Message

Here's a glimpse of the Prophet's life:

  • 570 AD - Muhammad son of Abd Allah and Amina was born with no father in the town of Mecca, and was entrusted to a wet nurse named Halima at early age following the traditions
  • 575 AD - Muhammad was around 6 years old when he was left in his paternal grandfather, Abdul Al-Mutallib's care, because of his mother's death
  • 578 AD - 3 years later, his grandfather dies too, leaving him in Abu Talib's - his paternal uncle- protection
  • 594 AD - During his teens, Muhammad PBUH worked as a shepherd and then as a merchant until he was recognized for his honesty and truthfulness and nicknamed "Al-amine/The trustworthy"
  • 595 AD - Impressed by Muhammad's honesty and character, Khadija(A wealthy female-merchant under whom Muhammad worked) eventually proposed marriage. Him being twenty-five and her nearly forty didn't interrupt them from living happily and felling in love with each other
  • ..609 AD - Unlike his people (Quraych tribe) and being a monotheist, Muhammad never bowed to a statue and was always meditating and admiring the beauty of this world ine the cave of Hira
  • 610 AD - Muhammad receives his first revelation by Allah through angel Gabriel. This experience shook the Prophet to the core, it took some time for him to talk about it outside the family
  • 613 AD - after similar experiences, Muhammad PBUH finally began to reveal the message to his close family, and friends, then to the rest of Quraych. His tribe didn't approve of it and wanted to stop him at all costs
  • 622 AD - During the last decade, his uncle and wife's died. Muhammad was left alone, with no protection him and his followers were belittled and attacked verbally and physically, which pushed him to travel to Yathrib where he had more followers
  • 629 AD - in this period of time, Yathrib was named Al-Maddinah -The city of the Prophet- and had the first mosque in islam, and Muhammad built an army of believers to protect themselves from their enemies
  • 630 AD - The Conquest of Mecca: after Quraych violated the terms of peace, the Prophet took his army to them and took control of Mecca without causing any bloodshed and even forgave everyone who harmed him and his followers before
  • 632 AD - The Greatest man in history dies: after spending the better part of his life spreading Allah's message, and performing a last pilgrimage*. Muhammad dies because of illness, leaving behind his followers and closest friends to spread the message further
